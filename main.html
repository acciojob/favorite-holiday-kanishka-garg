Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood. It is a perfect place to relax, explore, and enjoy with friends and family.

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for its lively atmosphere and water sports.
  2. Calangute Beach – Known as the "Queen of Beaches".
  3. Fort Aguada – A historic fort with amazing sea views.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A UNESCO World Heritage site.
  5. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – A breathtaking natural waterfall.
  • Goan Fish Curry – A spicy and flavorful seafood dish.
  • Prawn Balchão – A tangy and spicy prawn preparation.
  • Bebinca – A traditional Goan dessert.
  • Chicken Xacuti – A rich and aromatic curry.
  • Feni – A local alcoholic drink made from cashew.
